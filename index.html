About Us

1947, August 15th. A day every Indian remembers and celebrates. It was this same day the founder and Director of Azad Company group Mr. M S Azad was born. The same reason his father Ismail sahib named his firstborn son, Azad which means freedom. Azad being the sole proprietor of the company launched many new innovative products in the market. The product line included many kinds of squashes, various pickles, rosewater of good quality and smell, many types of juices, Curry masala powders and Soft drinks. Along with this a factory that employs almost 50 workers was formed.
